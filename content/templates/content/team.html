APA Racing

 

MARATHON RACING

The APA race team compete in marathon events in their 41ft Pantera boat powered by two 1025 HP Mercury Racing engines. APA founder Martin Lai mans the throttles whilst rising powerboat talent Charlotte Camsey steers the boat. This year the team has been joined by Tom Williams-Hawke who will navigate on the gruelling offshore courses. The challenges of offshore racing are very different to those of inshore circuit racing. APA engineer Mark Tribe takes care of the team's equipment and organises the logistics as they move the boat from event to event.

The team race around the UK and a list of upcoming events can be found at the bottom of this page. The legendary Pantera makes an impression in any marina, if you spot it then please go and say hello to the team. They are always keen to talk about the APA, powerboats or life in general.

 

CIRCUIT RACING

The APA races on inshore circuits across Asia in their custom built Shakespeare boats with 50 HP Tohatsu engines. As part of the APA race team APA talent Charlotte Camsey races in other circuit racing events. This year she is competing in one of our boats in the GT Monohull Light category of the UK Circuit Racing Association Championships (CPA). Charlotte races in an APA Shakespeare hull with a 70 HP Yamaha engine. As well as the CPA Charlotte races in local events in the South West, and international events across Europe.
